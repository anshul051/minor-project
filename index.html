The Sense-Predict-Act Model

A simple yet powerful approach to intelligent automation.

1. Sense & Connect

A capacitive soil sensor continuously gathers moisture data. The ESP32 microcontroller then transmits this information to the cloud in real-time.

2. Predict & Analyze

Our Python-based machine learning model analyzes historical data patterns to forecast the soil's moisture level for the next 24 hours.

3. Act & Automate

If the AI predicts moisture will drop below a critical threshold, it proactively sends a command via MQTT to activate the pump, ensuring the plant is watered just in time.

About This Project

This project was born from a desire to create a smarter, more efficient solution for plant care. It moves beyond simple timers and reactive sensors by leveraging the power of predictive analytics. By building a low-cost IoT device combined with a time-series forecasting model, Hydro-AI offers a glimpse into the future of automated, data-driven horticulture. It's not just about convenience; it's about water conservation and creating the perfect environment for plants to thrive.
